Types of Heart Disease

Heart disease encompasses various conditions that affect the heart...

Common types include:

  • Coronary artery disease (CAD)
  • Heart attack (Myocardial infarction)
  • Heart failure
  • Arrhythmias
  • Valvular heart disease

Each type has its own causes, symptoms, and treatment options...

Living with Heart Disease

If you're living with heart disease, there are steps you can take to manage your condition and improve your quality of life...

  • Follow a heart-healthy diet
  • Engage in regular physical activity
  • Quit smoking
  • Manage stress
  • Take medications as prescribed

Consult with your healthcare provider for personalized advice and treatment...
